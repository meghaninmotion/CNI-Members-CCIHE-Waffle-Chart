# CNI-Members-CCIHE-Waffle-Chart
A prototype mapping of CNI members by Carnegie Classification of Institutions of Higher Education for exploration. 

This waffle chart, or grid visualization, represents the membership of CNI and a breakdown of those members by their Carnegie Classification. The grid totals 100% and compresses total membership to a percentage of membership based on classification.

More details on the organizations can be found at https://www.cni.org/about-cni/membership/members. 

Details about the Carnegie Classification methods and historical changes can be found at https://carnegieclassifications.acenet.edu/carnegie-classification/classification-methodology/2025-institutional-classification/. This prototype is not an endorsement of the methods or effects of these research and teaching classifications.

This map was developed by pairing with Claude AI to develop html and is presented for exploration. It can be further developed with your feedback and suggestions. Please share those thoughts - and report errors - as [pull requests](https://github.com/meghaninmotion/CNI-Members-CCIHE-Waffle-Chart/pulls)!
